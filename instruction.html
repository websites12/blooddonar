Donor Instructions

You should not be suffering from any of the following diseases or taking medicines for them
1.Hepatitis B, C
2.AIDS # Diabetes (are you under medication currently?)
3.Fits/ Convulsions (are you under medication currently?)
4.Cancer # Leprosy or any other infectious diseases
5.Any allergies (Only if you are suffering from severe symptoms)
6.Hemophilia/ Bleeding problems
7.Kidney disease
8.Heart disease
9.Hormonal disorders
10.Any other type of Jaundice (within 5 years)
11.Tuberculosis (within 2 years)
12.Chicken Pox (within 1 year)
13.Malaria (within 1 year)
14.Organ Transplant (within one year)
15.Blood Transfusion (within the last 6 months)
16.Pregnancy (within the last 6 months)
17.Blood Donation (within the last 3 months)
18.Major Surgery (within the last 3 months)
19.Small Pox Vaccination (within the last 3weeks)
20.Hemoglobin deficiency / Anemia (recently)
21.Drastic weight loss (recently)
